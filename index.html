Microwave brigadeiro

You can add semisweet chocolate or even colorful sprinkles to vary the flavor and presentation. It's delicious! This recipe is super easy to make and will leave you wanting more. Brazilian recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of condensed milk;
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder (you can use chocolate powder, but cocoa powder gives a more intense flavor);
  • 1 tablespoon of margarine or butter;
  • Chocolate sprinkles (optional, for topping);

Preparation method:

  1. Mix the ingredients: In a microwave-safe bowl, add the condensed milk, cocoa powder and margarine.
  2. Microwave: Place the bowl in the microwave for 30 seconds. Remove and stir well. Return to the microwave for another 30 seconds and stir again.
  3. Cook for another 1-2 minutes: Repeat the process of heating for 30 seconds and stirring until the mixture starts to come away from the sides of the bowl. This is what indicates that the brigadeiro is ready.
  4. Let it cool: When the brigadeiro is ready, remove it from the microwave and let it cool for a few minutes.
  5. Shape the brigadeiros: With your hands greased with butter, make small balls with the brigadeiro mixture. If you prefer, roll them in sprinkles to cover them.
  6. Serve: After it has cooled, just serve and enjoy!
